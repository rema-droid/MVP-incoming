## 2025-01-24 - [Clear Search Pattern]
**Learning:** All search inputs (Sidebar, Marketplace, Mobile) should provide a conditional 'Clear' button (using an 'X' icon) when text is entered to improve usability on touch devices and desktop alike. Consistent placement and accessible ARIA labels are key.
**Action:** Always check if search inputs have a quick reset mechanism and ensure proper right-padding to avoid text overlap.

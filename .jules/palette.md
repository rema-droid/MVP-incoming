## 2025-05-14 - Conditional Clear Search Buttons
**Learning:** Implementing conditional "Clear" buttons (using an 'X' icon) on search inputs significantly improves usability, especially for touch and mobile users who otherwise have to backspace repeatedly. Providing an `aria-label` ensures this feature is accessible to screen reader users.
**Action:** Always include a conditional "Clear" button and appropriate right padding (`pr-9` or `pr-10`) for search inputs in this design system to prevent text overlap with the absolute-positioned icon.
